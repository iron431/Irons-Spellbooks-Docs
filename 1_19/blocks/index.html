Blocks

Alchemist Cauldron

Can be used to brew potions, craft elixirs, recycle scrolls, and upgrade ink. If a mob or player is placed in it while there is water, blood vials can also be produced. It has 4 input and 4 output slots, and must have water in it to activate. Right click to place a valid item into the input slots, and right click with a glass bottle to extract an output item.

Arcane Anvil

Used to upgrade scrolls, upgrade equipment, and imbue weapons

Arcane Debris

The magical Overworld equivalent of ancient debris, it can be found below y = -38, unexposed to air. Requires a netherite pickaxe to harvest. Used to smelt Arcane Salvage

Armor Pile

Found scattered throughout Ancient Battlegrounds. Harvesting with a diamond pickaxe will cause an Ancient Knight to spawn.

Firefly Jar

Inscription Table

Used to slot spells from scrolls into spell books for repeated use.

Pedestal

Unused block

Scroll Forge

Used to craft scrolls. The ink used determines the rarity of the spells. The level will always be the lowest level of that rarity. You cannot use an ink that is less rare than the minimum rarity of the scroll.
